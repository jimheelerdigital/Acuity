# Play Console — Short description

**Field name in Play Console:** App details → Short description
**Limit:** 80 characters
**Status:** Drafted 2026-06-03. Paste verbatim into Play Console.

---

## Primary choice (80 / 80)

```
Your nightly brain dump. Sixty seconds of talking. We pull out what mattered.
```

77 characters. Maya-voice. Names the trigger ("nightly"), the action ("sixty seconds of talking"), and what the app does with it ("pull out what mattered"). No banned words. No "AI-powered." No "journal."

## Why the iOS subtitle isn't a 1:1 port

iOS subtitle (`Your nightly brain dump`) is 22 characters — far under both stores' caps. Play Console's 80-char short description is closer to iOS's 170-char promotional text in role. So this doc adapts the iOS promotional text (`Your nightly brain dump, listened to. Sixty seconds of talking. We pull out your tasks, track your mood, and write you a weekly report about the patterns you can't see.`) into a 77-char version that fits.
